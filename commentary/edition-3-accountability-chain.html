The Accountability Chain
Three Fractures

At each stage, the system produces the formal output it requires.
The substance thins.
Formal Output
Substantive Reality
Ed. 1
Deemed decision
Statutory deadline expires. The FOI Act deems a refusal to have occurred.
No decision made
No assessment of the request. No exercise of judgement. No reasons.
Ed. 2
Statement of reasons
Agency issues a decision with reasons under the FOI Act.
Reasons inadequate
Factors listed but not weighed. Assertion substituted for analysis.
Ed. 3
Review queue
IC review application lodged. Matter enters the statutory review process.
Review cannot reach
Caseload pressure. Reviews dormant for years. Deficient decisions persist untested.
Each stage preserves form. The substance thins.
