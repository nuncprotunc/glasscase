GlassCase

Making law visible
Turning hidden processes into public understanding. Making law visible.

GlassCase makes the operation of law and administration visible, measurable and teachable. We translate opaque legal and bureaucratic systems into interactive visualisations that reveal how law is applied, not just what it says.

Mission

Make decisions and procedures legible to the public: show the path of a right, the points of discretion and delay, and what review looks like in practice.

Problem

Citizens don't see what happens after lodging an FOI. Policymakers can't visualise where discretion lives. Students learn doctrine, not process. The result: low trust and slow reform.

Fragmented systems

Multiple laws, agencies and timelines

Rules live in Acts, policies and guidance spread across sites. No shared map, no shared language.

Opaque pathways

What actually happens after you click submit

Process steps, consultations and exemptions sit out of view, so people can't anticipate outcomes.

Solution

Open, interactive tools that let people see, explore and learn real administrative pathways.
